climate

Kerala enjoys the three major seasons- Summer,monsoon and winter. It is a tropical land with,generally,pleasant climate.Winter is the peak season for kerala tourism.

Agriculture

Agriculture is the state's main economic activity.Kerala's principal cash crops are rubber,coffee and tea as well as cardamom,cashew nut,coconut,ginger and pepper.The major food crops are rice,pulses,sorghum. Commercial poultry farming is well-developed.

Forest

The forests yield valuable timbrs such as rosewood,teak.Kerala's woodlands supply industrial raw materials such as bamboo,wood pulp,gums and resins.

Resources and Power

Kerala lacks major reserves of fossil fuels.There are moderate deposits of rutile,monazite,all of which are found in beach sands.The state is especially known for its high-quality kaolin.It has great hydroelectric potential.

Education

Kearal has one of the most advanced educational systems and highest levels of literacy in India. There are polytechnical,industrial training institutes,arts and science colleges too.
